FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Where is the store based?

Store is based in Singapore.

Do you accept orders placed outside of Singapore?

Yes, we sure do!

Where do you ship your products from, and do you ship to my country?

Products are shipped from Singapore and worldwide shipping is offered.

If you want to purchase products but your country is not listed in the shipping options, please send me an e-mail here and we’ll sort it out!

How long will it take to receive my order?

Packages are sent via airmail through Singpost, no tracking. Most domestic orders (Singapore) take 1-2 working days to arrive,

to everywhere else in the world the delivery times vary between 2 and 12 weeks.

When will my order be shipped?

We ship once a week, so your order will be shipped 2-7 days after you placed order.

What if something is wrong with my order?

If you have a problem with an order from this store, please contact the store directly through the Contact > Email page

Are you going to sell prints of your latest illustration?

I’m always open for suggestions and might consider making prints or other products of my illustrations. I might end up making it available if people show interest!

Do you do commissions?

Currently it is only limited to local orders only. If you are in Singapore and wish to have a commission for me, feel free to send an email!
